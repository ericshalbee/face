Best Practices to Avoid Issues

Prevention is better than cure. Follow these guidelines to minimize problems.

Use Real Information

Fake names or profiles increase the chance of being flagged. Always use your real name and accurate information.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security. Go to Settings > Security > 2FA to set it up.

Comply With Community Standards

Avoid hate speech, nudity, graphic content, and spam. Review Facebook's Community Guidelines regularly.

Ad Policy Compliance

Read Facebook's ad rules before running campaigns. Avoid sensational content, incomplete landing pages, and misleading claims.

Monitor Business Quality

Check Account Quality regularly to catch issues early and maintain good standing.

Tips for Faster Support Resolution

Maximize your chances of getting quick, effective help

Be Clear & Detailed

When reporting problems, include:

  • • Device type and operating system
  • • Screenshots of the issue
  • • Step-by-step description
  • • When the problem started
  • • Error messages (exact wording)

Respond Quickly

If Facebook replies asking for more information:

  • • Respond within 24-48 hours
  • • Provide all requested documents
  • • Answer all questions completely
  • • Check your Support Inbox regularly

Avoid Multiple Tickets

Duplicate tickets can slow down the process:

  • • Submit one ticket per issue
  • • Don't resubmit if no immediate response
  • • Use Support Inbox to track progress
  • • Wait for the review period to complete

Use Business Support

If eligible, business support is faster:

  • • More direct communication
  • • Shorter response times
  • • Specialized knowledge
  • • Available during business hours

When You Can't Get Support

Sometimes you may not see Live Chat or email options. This usually means:

  • • Your ad spend is too low
  • • Account doesn't meet eligibility criteria
  • • Support not available in your region

Workaround: Use community forums like Reddit's r/facebook or Meta's official Facebook Business Community.

Facebook vs Meta Support

Facebook Support

Help related to your Facebook account, feed, messages, and groups.

Meta Support

Covers all platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp) and business tools.

Most support now routes through Meta, especially for business-related issues.
